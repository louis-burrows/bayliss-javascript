Company overview

Nick Bayliss has over 40 years experience of providing high quality design, manufacture, installation and restoration of stained and painted glass and leaded lights, which includes the following...

  1. Traditional and modern stained and painted glass windows and doors
  2. Traditional leaded light windows and doors
  3. Restoration of period timber, painted stained glass and wrought iron windows
  4. Traditional hand crafted fixtures and fittings
  5. Protective metal guards to help prevent damage to vulnerable glass
  6. Triple glazing to original and new leaded lights, to conform with current building regulations
  7. In addition, our skilled craftsmen can repair stained glass, painted glass and leaded lights from any period or design, and manufacture reproductions to match the originals
